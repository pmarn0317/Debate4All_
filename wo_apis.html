Scheduling

Scheduling is easy. You can either fill out the following form to add your own debate or scroll down to where it says "available" to find debates that have been added by others and add yourself by clicking the "add" button. Once you add yourself or someone adds themselves to your debate, you will get each other's contact information. Use that contact info to schedule a debate.
